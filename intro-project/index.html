Hey there, I'm Cindy, and I'm excited to introduce you to my world in Crystal Lake. Let me share with you some of the things that make me who I am.

    Art Enthusiast: Art has always been a way to express myself, my thoughts, inspirations and I love seaiing different forms of art.Whether it's the classic charm of putting pencil to paper, or the magic of digital artistry, I find joy in every stroke. My journey into digital art took an exciting turn when I saved up enough to snag an iPad during a sale. The game-changer, however, was the day I got my hands on the Apple Pencil a few months later. With Procreate as my canvas and the Apple Pencil as my brush, I've explored the boundless world of digital art with newfound precision and creativity.
    Gamer at Heart: When I'm not lost in the world of art, you'll often find me in front of a screen, diving into the thrilling realm of video games. One of my absolute favorites is "Five Nights at Freddy's." It's a bit spooky, but that's what makes it so incredibly fun. I thrive on games that send shivers down my spine! I also have a soft spot for those retro-styled horror games, especially the ones created by Puppet Combo. They bring back the eerie nostalgia of a bygone era, and I can't get enough of them.
    Music Enthusiast: Music is the soundtrack of my life. I'm open to exploring just about any genre, whether it's the electrifying beats of rock, the mesmerizing rhythms of electronic music, or the timeless melodies of classical compositions. Music has a unique power to evoke emotions, and I cherish every note. However, there's one genre I tend to steer clear of, and that's country music. It's just not my cup of tea.
This website is my canvas although to be honest for an artist anything is a canvas, a platform where I invite you to explore my world of creativity and passion. Here, you'll find a collection of my art, insights into the captivating world of video games, and maybe even a music recommendation or two. I hope you'll enjoy delving into my interests, and who knows, you might just discover something that resonates with you. Thank you for visiting, and let's embark on this creative journey together!
