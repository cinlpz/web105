My Music Intrest

I've always been surrounded by different kinds of music, like songs in Spanish, English, and even languages like Japanese, Chinese, and Korean. It all started way back in 2009 when I heard my first Korean song called "Mirotic" by TVXQ. It was on a CD that my older sister got from her friends who liked sharing cool music.Believe it or not, I still listen to that same song today! I couldn't understand why some people liked music with loud screaming sounds.

Then, I began to like other styles too, such as rap, reggaeton, R&B, and even classical pieces. And you know what's funny? These days, I've actually grown to like metal and rock music.
