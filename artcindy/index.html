About the artist

My name is Cindy Lopez, and I'm 18 years old. I love creating art, and I do it as a freelance artist. I enjoy working with various forms of art, including photography, creative writing, digital art, and traditional art. Ever since I was young, I've been fascinated by art. I used to watch speed art videos on YouTube and animatics, which inspired me to pursue the cartoon-style art. Right now, I specialize in creating art in a cartoonized style. I find my inspiration in the work of other artists and the media I enjoy. When I draw, I usually design characters and create their unique worlds. It's something I really love doing. I'm also interested in digital painting and want to get better at it. Even though I haven't been part of big art exhibitions yet, I did take part in art shows at my high school. I'm always learning and practicing to improve my art skills. I work on my art almost every day because I believe that practice makes perfect. I'm passionate about my art, and I'm excited to keep learning and growing as an artist.
